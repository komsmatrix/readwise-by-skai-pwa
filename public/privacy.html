Privacy Policy

Effective: June 16, 2026 Last updated: June 16, 2026 Governed by RA 10173

Short version: We collect only what we need to run your study plan. We never sell your data. You can request deletion anytime by emailing hello@readwisebyskai.com.

1. Who We Are

Readwise by Skai ("Readwise", "we", "our") is a Philippine-based educational technology platform operating at readwisebyskai.com. We provide a national exam preparation platform for Philippine licensure examinees and TESDA qualification reviewees.

For privacy concerns, contact us at: hello@readwisebyskai.com

2. What Data We Collect

Data TypeWhat We CollectWhy
Account Full name, email address To create your account and send access keys
Exam Info Target exam (e.g. LET), exam date, study mode To build your personalized study plan
Study Activity Cards reviewed, answers given, session times, readiness scores, topic health To power spaced repetition, recommendations, and your Readiness Score
Payment Amount paid, payment reference (via PayMongo) To verify your purchase and issue your access key
Referral Referral code used (if any) To credit the referring agent and apply your discount
Device/Technical Browser type, theme preference (stored locally) To remember your settings between sessions

We do not collect: government IDs, PRC registration numbers, financial account details, or sensitive personal information as defined under RA 10173.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver your personalized study plan and Readiness Score
  • To schedule spaced repetition reviews based on your performance
  • To send your access key and important account emails
  • To process and verify your payment through PayMongo
  • To improve the platform based on aggregate (non-personal) usage patterns
  • To respond to your feedback and support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling for third parties, or any purpose unrelated to your exam preparation.

4. Who We Share Data With

ServicePurposeData Shared
Supabase Database and file storage All account and study data (stored securely)
PayMongo Payment processing Name, email, payment amount
Resend Transactional email Name and email (for sending access keys and reminders)
Vercel Web hosting and API Request logs (standard hosting data, retained briefly)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Our agents receive only aggregate referral counts — never individual student data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers. We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure each student can only access their own data. Access keys, payment secrets, and API credentials are stored server-side only and never exposed to the browser.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by RA 10173.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Active accounts: Retained for as long as your account is active
  • Trial sessions: Retained for 90 days after trial expiry
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Philippine tax law
  • Deleted accounts: Personal data removed within 30 days of deletion request

7. Your Rights Under RA 10173

As a data subject under the Philippine Data Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Object — object to processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Complain — file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (privacy.gov.ph)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@readwisebyskai.com. We will respond within 15 business days.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

Readwise uses browser local storage (not cookies) to remember your theme preference and session state. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers unless you are logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Readwise is intended for users 18 years and older (exam applicants (board exams and TESDA qualifications)). We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at hello@readwisebyskai.com and we will delete the account promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform grows. When we make significant changes, we will notify active students via email and post an announcement in the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
